Guru Session 2021 ( MOVE - Evolving in difficult times)

The global pandemic left everyone startle of what next? Businesses were short down, cities locked, schools closed, religious centers empty and for once humanity felt helpless. What a time it is. People everywhere are angry, suspicious, and distrustful... often with good reason. And yet, might it be possible to emerge from the current fiery moment stronger than before? Wiser? Happier? With the careful combination of visionary ideas, strategic planning, hard work and lucky timing, we think so. At this very special Guru Session, we'll celebrate the clear-eyed and the bold of heart, those who don't shy away from the most intractable-seeming problems but rather step toward them to make the case that, even now, hope is possible. Guru Session 2021 is packaged to help our participants take the next BIG MOVE in embracing the new normal. The conference will provide a clear compass on how anyone from any sector can "evolve in difficult times".
